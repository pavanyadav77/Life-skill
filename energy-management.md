# Energy Management Reflection

## Question 1: Activities that make me relax (Calm Quadrant)

Some activities that help me feel calm and relaxed are:

- Listening to soft or slow music
- Going for a quiet walk without using my phone
- Sitting and talking with friends for a few minutes
- Taking a cold shower
- walking before sleeping
- Talking to someone I trust
- Watching series TMKOC

These activities help me slow down and feel peaceful.

---

## Question 2: When do I enter the Stress Quadrant?

I usually feel stressed:

- When deadlines are very close
- When I have too many tasks at the same time
- When I am learning something difficult
- When I overthink about results and the future
- When I don’t get proper sleep
- When I compare myself with others

Sometimes stress helps me work better, but if it stays too long, I feel tired and irritated.

---

## Question 3: How do I know I am in the Excitement Quadrant?

I understand I am in excitement when:

- I feel motivated and curious
- I enjoy doing the task
- Time passes very fast
- I feel challenged but not afraid
- I feel positive and energetic
- I look forward to completing the work

In this state, I feel fully involved and happy.

---

## Question 4: Sleep is Your Superpower (Key Points Only)

- Sleep improves memory
- Sleep helps in learning and focus
- Poor sleep increases stress
- Sleep improves immunity
- Lack of sleep affects decision-making
- Sleep balances emotions
- Good sleep improves overall health

---

## Question 5: Ideas I can implement to sleep better

- Sleep and wake up at the same time daily
- Avoid using the phone before sleeping
- Keep my room dark and quiet
- Avoid caffeine at night
- Avoid heavy meals late at night
- Try to relax my mind before sleeping

---

## Question 6: Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise (Key Points Only)

- Exercise improves brain function
- Reduces stress and anxiety
- Improves memory
- Increases focus
- Boosts mood
- Helps in better sleep
- Increases energy levels

---

## Question 7: Steps I can take to exercise more

- I will walk for 10–15 minutes daily.
- I will perform yoga for 10 minutes every day.
- I will try to play a sport on weekends.
- I will remind myself that a small effort is better than no effort.
- I will not aim for perfection, just regular movement.
